How this gets live

Programs — from the P&I Task Tracker (Smartsheet). Clients, launch dates, promo windows, and live links pull from the sheet (Crain Brand = P&I, Status = Launched). Webinar registrations come from the sheet too.
White-paper leads — from Jotform. Counts are matched from the Jotform analytics export (submissions per form). Today this is a manual snapshot; once the P&I Jotform workspace is connected, these update automatically.
N/A items. Sponsored content and quick-take videos are ungated awareness plays — no lead form, so no lead count.
Refresh cadence. A daily 7:00 AM task refreshes programs from Smartsheet and republishes the page; lead counts refresh when a new Jotform export is provided (or automatically once Jotform is connected).

Sources: Pensions & Investments Task Tracker (Smartsheet) for programs & webinar registrations; Jotform analytics export (Jun 10, 2026) for white-paper lead counts. Built off the Ad Solutions Team Dashboard pattern.
