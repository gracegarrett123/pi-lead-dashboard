How this gets live

Programs — from the P&I Task Tracker (Smartsheet). Clients, launch dates, promo windows, and live links pull from the sheet (Crain Brand = P&I, Status = Launched). Webinar registrations come from the sheet too.
White-paper leads — from the P&I TLP Tracker (Smartsheet). Submission counts are maintained by the campaigns team and matched to each program by Arc live link. They now refresh automatically from Smartsheet.
Refresh cadence. A daily 7:00 AM task refreshes programs and webinar registrations from Smartsheet, pulls the latest white-paper submission counts from the P&I TLP Tracker, and republishes the page.

Sources: Pensions & Investments Task Tracker (Smartsheet) for programs; Webinar Registrants P&I report (Smartsheet) for webinar registrations; P&I TLP Tracker | Launched Campaigns (Smartsheet) for white-paper submission counts. Built off the Ad Solutions Team Dashboard pattern.
